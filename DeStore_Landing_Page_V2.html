How it works
Three layers. One permanent record.
DeStore is the infrastructure underneath circular trade. Brands, retailers, resale channels, and recyclers all plug in. The product carries its own record.
1
Create a DPP
Paste a product URL or fill in details manually. DeStore generates a Digital Product Passport with a GS1 Digital Link QR code. One DPP per product sold.
Brand side

Consumer scans the QR with any phone camera. The DPP landing page shows provenance, materials, sustainability data, and full ownership history. One scan, full story.

Infrastructure
Built for the full lifecycle
Every feature exists to move a product through its circular life — from manufacture through every owner to end-of-life.
◈
Digital Product Passports
One DPP per product sold. Standard (hosted, ESPR compliant) or on-chain (sovereign, premium). Provenance, materials, sustainability data, care instructions — all in one permanent record.
GS1 Digital Link QR
Industry-standard QR codes that resolve via your branded domain. Scan with any phone camera — no app required. qr.yourbrand.com/01/09506000130017
Zero crypto UX
Consumers sign in with email, Google, or Apple. Smart accounts deploy behind the scenes. No seed phrases, no gas fees, no wallets. The infrastructure is invisible.
EU ESPR ready
Built for the EU Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation. Textiles are the first mandatory DPP wave (~mid-2028). DeStore is ahead of the compliance curve.
Digital wallet cards
Apple Wallet and Google Wallet passes for every product. Consumers carry proof of ownership in the same place as their boarding passes and credit cards.
Circular actions
Sell, gift, recycle, return — built into every DPP. Ownership transfers with USDC atomic swaps. Every circular event is permanently recorded.
White-label domain
Connect your own domain. Products resolve under your brand — qr.yourbrand.com. Full DNS wizard with CNAME verification, SSL, and resolver health checks.
Webhook integrations
Connect Shopify, Squarespace, WooCommerce. Auto-issue DPPs at point of sale. n8n workflow engine for custom automation. Your existing stack, our circular rails.
