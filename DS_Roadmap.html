◈ Soft Launch Vehicle
DPP Foundation Program
The Foundation program is how brands get started before compliance deadlines. They get real tools, real barcodes, and real digital experiences for their products — plus a team trained and ready to activate the full ESPR workflow when the time comes.
GS1 Barcodes
Every product gets a working GTIN via the DeStore GS1 prefix. No GS1 membership needed. Scan-ready QR codes generated instantly.
Digital Experiences
Customers scan the QR and land on a branded product page — materials, origin, sustainability story. The brand owns the post-purchase relationship.
Custom QR Design
Branded QR codes with custom colours, logos, and styles. Templates for consistency across product lines. Looks like the brand, not a barcode.
AI Data Capture
The AI onboarding captures product data conversationally. 10 minutes per product. Structured and ready for when ESPR compliance activates.
Staff Training
Onboarding packages teach the brand's team to manage products, generate QR codes, and run the DPP workflow independently. Self-sufficiency is the goal.
DPP Workflow Practice
The Foundation workflow lets teams build muscle memory with the 6-node pipeline. When the ESPR workflow activates, they already know the system.
Analytics
See who's scanning, where, and when. Understand how customers engage with products before and after purchase.
Platform Integrations
Connect to Shopify, Squarespace, and existing systems via webhooks. DPP data flows into the brand's existing workflow, not alongside it.
The pitch to brands: "Get GS1 barcodes and digital product experiences on your products today. Train your team on the DPP system now. When EU compliance lands, you're already live — not starting from scratch."
