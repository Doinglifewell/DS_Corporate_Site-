Invite Team Member

Add a team member to EcoThreads. They'll be able to create products, run AI onboarding, and manage DPP workflows.
Team members can create products, run AI onboarding, configure DPP workflows, and issue DPPs. They cannot manage billing, invite compliance officers, or change brand settings.

Invite Compliance Officer

Invite an external reviewer to verify your DPP Preliminary Reports. Their approval is permanently recorded with a digital signature and on-chain event.
The officer will receive an email with a review link. They can view reports, add reviewer notes, and approve or request changes. They cannot edit product data, create products, or access billing. Their approval is captured with timestamp, SHA-256 hash, digital signature, and on-chain Soneium event.

Edit Role

Change the role for this team member.
Changing a member's role takes effect immediately. Brand Owner role grants full access including billing, invites, and brand settings. Team Member role restricts to product creation and DPP workflow only.

Remove Team Member

This action removes their access to EcoThreads on DeStore.
What happens:
  • They lose access to all EcoThreads data on DeStore
  • They can no longer create products or generate reports
  • Products they created remain unchanged
  • Their DeStore account continues to exist

Revoke Compliance Officer Access
