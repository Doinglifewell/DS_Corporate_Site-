✕ REACH / SVHC — What is this? REACH (Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation and Restriction of Chemicals) is EU regulation EC 1907/2006. SVHC = Substances of Very High Concern. If your product contains any SVHC above 0.1% by weight, you must disclose them in the DPP and notify ECHA's SCIP database.

✕ Purchase URL The URL where this product can be bought. Used in schema.org/Offer structured data so search engines can show a "Buy" action directly in results. Leave blank to omit the buy action from structured data.
✕ Energy Efficiency Class — What is this? The EU Energy Label (Regulation 2021/341) requires products like appliances, electronics, tyres, lamps and displays to carry an A–G energy class. Class A is most efficient; G is least. The label must appear on the product, packaging, and DPP.

Not applicable for gemstones, raw materials, and non-energy-related products.
