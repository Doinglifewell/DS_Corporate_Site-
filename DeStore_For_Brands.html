Getting Started

From zero to verified
in five steps.

No special knowledge required. No app for your customers to download. Standard digital printing — no special inks or NFC chips.

01

Create your brand account.

Sign in with Google. Set up your brand profile — name, logo, description, and GS1 company prefix if you have one. DeStore can help you register with GS1 Australia if needed.

5 minutes
02

Connect your subdomain.

Point your branded domain (e.g. qr.yourbrand.com) to the DeStore resolver via CNAME. Your customers see your brand on every scan — not ours. Full white-label infrastructure. Follow the setup wizard →

10 minutes
03

Create collections and add products.

Use the Collection Creator to upload product images, enter details, set quantities. Each product auto-generates unique GTIN-14 identifiers and GS1 Digital Links. Serialised down to every individual unit.

Depends on catalogue size
04

Print and apply to packaging.

Download your product registry and Laava Smart Fingerprint artwork. Print on labels, hang tags, certificates, or packaging using standard digital printing. Ship your products as normal.

Your production timeline
05

Go live — customers scan and verify.

Customers scan with any smartphone camera. They see your branded product page with verified provenance, ownership history, circular trade actions (trade, gift, recycle, return), and your brand story. You get real-time scan analytics across every market.

What's included

Everything in the box.

Every DeStore brand account includes the full circular trade toolkit.

⬡

Laava Smart Fingerprints.

Unique, unclonable optical fingerprints for every product unit. Secure authentication developed with CSIRO. Standard digital printing — no special inks required.

◎

Branded resolver.

White-label subdomain (qr.yourbrand.com) with Docker and Cloudflare Tunnel infrastructure. Customers see your brand on every scan. Set up your domain →

⊞

Collection Creator.

Self-serve tool to create product collections, upload images, set quantities, and auto-generate GTIN-14 codes with GS1 Digital Links. Export as JSON, CSV, or branded reports.

◈

Digital Product Passports.

A permanent record for every product. DPP Soulbound Token minted to your brand wallet on Base, Polygon, or Soneium. Metadata stored permanently on Arweave. Consumers verify with a single scan — zero crypto UX.

◉

Scan analytics dashboard.

Real-time data on every scan — location, device, time, engagement. Detect distribution anomalies, measure campaign performance, track global market penetration.

Circular trade actions.

Every DPP enables trade, gift, recycle, and return. Ownership transfers via USDC atomic swap on Thirdweb Marketplace V3. $0.01 per lifecycle event funds the B&B Treasury for future buyback-and-burn.

Digital wallet cards.

Apple Wallet and Google Wallet passes for every product. Consumers carry proof of ownership in the same place as their boarding passes and credit cards.

⬢

Product registry.

Google Sheets-based registry with automated n8n workflows. Product data, scan logs, DPP status, and config — all in one live spreadsheet your team already knows how to use.
