What a DPP actually does

A Digital Product Passport is not a PDF or a certificate. It's a living on-chain record that travels with your product — forever.

01
EU Market Access — Gone Without It
The ESPR regulation is already law. From July 2027, every product category entering the EU requires a machine-readable Digital Product Passport containing materials, origin, carbon footprint, repairability, and end-of-life data. No DPP — no sale. Australian brands who move now build a competitive advantage. Everyone else scrambles.
02
Counterfeits Die at the Scan
Every DeStore DPP pairs with a GS1 Digital Link QR code and Laava Smart Fingerprint™ — a physical authentication layer that can't be copied. Customers scan before they buy. Fakes fail instantly. Your brand is protected at first sale, resale, and every transfer in between.
03
Own the Full Product Lifecycle
Sell, resell, gift, return, recycle — every event permanently recorded and anchored to Ethereum mainnet. The DPP follows the product forever. And every time someone scans your QR code — at purchase, at resale, at a market stall — they land on your domain. Your brand. Your story. Not a third-party page. That's what separates a compliant DPP from a competitive one.
How it works

Every scan is a direct conversation between your product and your customer.

01
DPP Issued
Your product gets a unique Digital Product Passport — issued on Base, anchored to Ethereum mainnet. Materials, origin, carbon, ownership. Tamper-evident. Permanent.
02
Customer Scans
A GS1 Digital Link QR on the product or tag. No app required — just a phone camera. The scan instantly verifies authenticity and resolves to your domain. Not a third-party platform.
03
Permanent Record
Every scan, every ownership transfer, every lifecycle event is permanently recorded. A complete, tamper-evident chain of custody — visible to regulators, customers, and resellers. Forever.
04
You Own the Data
Who scanned. Where. When. First owner, second owner, third. Every resale, every return. Your dashboard. Your customers. Your product story — told on your terms, not Amazon's or Facebook's.
◈ RECORD VERIFIED
Every DPP is issued on Base (Coinbase's L2), and every batch is cryptographically anchored to Ethereum mainnet — the world's most battle-tested public ledger. Your product record is not stored in a database someone can delete. It lives on a public record that no company, government, or bad actor can alter or erase.
