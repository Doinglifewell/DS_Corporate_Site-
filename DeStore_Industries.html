Art & Cultural Heritage

Indigenous art, paintings and collectibles.

The trade in fake Indigenous Australian art is estimated at $200 million per year. Verified provenance combined with Smart Fingerprints protects artists, preserves culture, and empowers consumers to verify authenticity.

Case Study — Indigenous Art Authentication

Smart Fingerprints were applied to authorised jigsaw puzzles featuring artist Chern'ee Sutton's work, partnering with IndigiLedger. Consumers scan to verify authenticity and connect with the artist's story. Launched during NAIDOC Week, covered by ABC Radio and 7 News Brisbane.
